Polyurea Coating

Polyurea coating is an advanced protective coating technology offering superior durability, flexibility, and waterproofing. It is ideal for industrial, commercial, and infrastructure applications.

  • ✔ Works in high humidity & low temperatures
  • ✔ Extreme abrasion resistance
  • ✔ Impermeable waterproof membrane
  • ✔ High thickness build-up
  • ✔ Excellent chemical resistance
